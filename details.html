Dress Code

We want you to feel comfortable and look your best! Our wedding will be semi-formal/cocktail attire.

For Guests

Think elegant spring celebration in the Swedish countryside. Cocktail dresses, dressy separates, suits, or blazers with dress pants are all perfect. Ties are optional—comfort is key!

Please note: We kindly ask that you avoid jeans and overly casual attire.

June in Sweden

June weather in Sweden is generally pleasant with temperatures around 15-20°C (59-68°F). We recommend bringing a light jacket or wrap for the evening, as it can get cooler after sunset.

Travel & Transportation

Flying In

Most international guests will fly into Göteborg Landvetter Airport (GOT), which is Sweden's second-largest airport.

Airport: Göteborg Landvetter Airport (GOT)

Distance to Stenungsund: ~50 km (31 miles)

Travel Time: 45-60 minutes

Website: swedavia.com/landvetter

Getting to Stenungsund

By Bus (Most Economical)

Take the Flygbussarna airport coach to Gothenburg Central Station, then connect to a bus to Stenungsund via Västtrafik.

  • Total Time: ~90 minutes
  • Cost: ~200-300 SEK (~$20-30 USD)
  • Airport Bus: Flygbussarna (every 15-20 minutes)
  • Regional Bus: Västtrafik Journey Planner

Tip: Download the Västtrafik app for easy ticket purchasing and real-time schedules.

By Taxi (Most Convenient)

Taxis are readily available at the airport and offer direct service to Stenungsund.

  • Travel Time: 45-60 minutes
  • Estimated Cost: 600-900 SEK (~$60-90 USD)
  • Pre-book: Taxi Göteborg

Tip: Pre-booking often guarantees a fixed price and can be cheaper than hailing at the airport.

Car Rental

If you're planning to explore the area, renting a car offers the most flexibility.

  • Major Rental Companies: Available at Landvetter Airport (Hertz, Avis, Europcar, etc.)

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the weather like in June?

June is a beautiful time in Sweden! Expect temperatures around 15-20°C (59-68°F) during the day. Evenings can be cooler, so we recommend bringing a light jacket or wrap. Rain is possible, so a small umbrella might be handy.

What's the dress code?

Semi-formal/cocktail attire. Think elegant and comfortable! See our Dress Code section above for more details.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We'd love for you to be fully present during our ceremony! We'll have a professional photographer capturing every moment. We kindly ask that you put away your phones and cameras during the ceremony—but feel free to snap away at the reception!

What language will the ceremony be in?

The ceremony will be conducted in English, making it accessible for all our international guests. Some elements may include Swedish touches to honor Gabriella's heritage.

Will there be transportation between the hotel and venue?

Stenungsbad Yacht Club is just a few minutes from Villa Vanahem. We'll provide more details about transportation options closer to the date.

What time should I arrive?

Please plan to arrive at least 15-20 minutes before the ceremony start time (14:00) on Saturday, June 6th. This gives you time to find parking, get settled, and enjoy the venue before we begin.

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will be held outdoors, and the reception will be indoors.
