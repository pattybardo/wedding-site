Our Story

Our love story began at Josephine Falls on Australia's east coast, where a chance encounter turned into destiny. We reconnected at a hostel in Mission Beach over free wine, and from there, we kept finding our way back to each other (even dragging our travel partners along just for another glimpse).

After a year of long distance (thanks, Covid), we knew this was it. Four years later, Patrick proposed at a beach sunset in Malaga. He forgot to hit record and got an "Awww" instead of a "yes," but somehow, it was perfect.

On November 8th, 2025, our world got even brighter when Benjamin Algot Bardo arrived. Now, we're ready to celebrate our journey so far and the adventure ahead with all of you by our side.
