Travel & Transportation

Flying In

Most international guests will fly into Göteborg Landvetter Airport (GOT), which is Sweden's second-largest airport.

Airport: Göteborg Landvetter Airport (GOT)

Distance to Stenungsund: ~50 km (31 miles)

Travel Time: 45-60 minutes

Website: swedavia.com/landvetter

Getting to Stenungsund

By Bus (Most Economical)

Take the Flygbussarna airport coach to Gothenburg Central Station, then connect to a bus to Stenungsund via Västtrafik.

  • Total Time: ~90 minutes
  • Cost: ~200-300 SEK (~$20-30 USD)
  • Airport Bus: Flygbussarna (every 15-20 minutes)
  • Regional Bus: Västtrafik Journey Planner

Tip: Download the Västtrafik app for easy ticket purchasing and real-time schedules.

Uber

Ubers are readily available at the airport and offer direct service to Stenungsund.

  • Travel Time: 45-60 minutes
  • Estimated Cost: 600-900 SEK (~$60-90 USD)
  • Pre-book: Taxi Göteborg

Tip: Pre-booking often guarantees a fixed price and can be cheaper than hailing at the airport.

Car Rental

If you're planning to explore beyond Gothenburg (such as the beautiful west coast archipelago, driving to Stockholm, or making your own way to Stenungsund), renting a car offers great flexibility.

  • Major Rental Companies: Available at Landvetter Airport (Hertz, Avis, Europcar, etc.)

Note: Public transport within and around Gothenburg is very convenient, so a car isn't necessary if you're staying in the city.

Things to Do in the Area

If you're extending your trip to Sweden, there are wonderful experiences waiting for you! Here are some of our favorite recommendations:

Liseberg Amusement Park

Scandinavia's largest amusement park right in Gothenburg! Perfect for families and thrill-seekers alike, with beautiful gardens, exciting rides, and great food. Especially magical if you're visiting during their Christmas market season.

Visit Liseberg

West Coast Archipelago

Explore the stunning Swedish west coast archipelago with its picturesque fishing villages, rocky islands, and fresh seafood. Take a boat tour, go kayaking, or simply enjoy the serene coastal beauty. Highlights include Marstrand, Smögen, and the Southern Gothenburg Archipelago.

Explore the Archipelago

Stockholm - A Larger Adventure

If you have extra time, Stockholm is about 5 hours away by train or a short flight from Gothenburg. Sweden's capital is built on 14 islands and offers world-class museums (Vasa Museum, ABBA Museum), the historic Gamla Stan (Old Town), beautiful architecture, and vibrant neighborhoods. It's well worth the journey!

Discover Stockholm
